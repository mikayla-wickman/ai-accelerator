What Each Prototype Solves Best

P1 — Smart Start
Best for new Pro onboarding and SMBs
When we want to reduce time-to-value and make HCP feel instantly relevant to a trade. Industry templates accelerate onboarding and reduce the blank slate problem.
HVAC Plumbing Cleaning New Pros
P2 — In Context
Best for CSRs and office managers working live
When the person setting up fields is the same person using them. Contextual creation removes the barrier of navigating to settings — the field appears exactly where it's needed.
Office Manager CSR Live Workflow
P3 — Control Center
Best for franchises, enterprise, and data-driven owners
When data standardization and reporting are the primary goals. Unblocks Neighborly key codes and ServiceMaster churn tracking — the franchise governance layer needs this surface.
Franchise Neighborly ServiceMaster
Synthesis note
These are not mutually exclusive. A final solution might combine P3's Settings table (for admin governance) with P2's contextual discovery empty state (for CSR adoption), seeded with P1's industry templates at first launch. The three directions are meant to surface what matters most to each user segment so we can make a deliberate tradeoff.
