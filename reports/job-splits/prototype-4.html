Job Split Defaults

This default applies automatically to all new jobs with multiple technicians. Owners and admins can override the split on any individual job from the Team tab on the job details page.

Default revenue split method
All to First Assigned
100% of revenue attributed to the first technician. Others receive 0%.
Marcus: 100% / Jamie: 0%
Equal Split
Revenue divided evenly among all assigned technicians at completion.
2 techs: 50% / 50%  ·  3 techs: 33% / 33% / 33%
Proportional to Time Logged ⏱
Revenue split based on hours clocked per tech. Requires time tracking.
Marcus 5.5h (69%) / Jamie 2.5h (31%) on an 8h job
No default — set manually per job
Jobs will show splits as "Pending" until an admin sets them in the Team tab.
Team tab will prompt admin on each completed job
Commission defaults
🔗 Link commission splits to revenue splits by default
When on, new jobs will start with commissions mirroring revenue splits. Admins can always decouple on individual jobs via the Team tab toggle.
⚠ Changes apply to new jobs only. Existing jobs with saved splits won't be affected unless manually updated from the Team tab.
